    Mission and Goals

    Our mission is to become a leading innovative waste management and recycling company in Zimbabwe, committed to continuous improvement for the company itself, our customers and the environment around us, supporting customers in complying with current /new legislation. With the support of our employees we will aim to grow the company incorporating Energy From Waste, leading too 100% recycling, whilst maintaining successful, long standing business partnerships with both our contractors and our customers.

    • to increase the production level so that we can export more quality product to earn the most profit.

    • to become successful and profitable paper production company comparing with others

    Vision and Values

    Our vision is to capture the whole paper recycling or that kind of business which is related to us both locally and regionally.

    • to achieve the best possible results for our clients.

    what we do

    Recycling is the practice of repurposing waste resources to create new products.All recovered paper material is supplied to our Baobab paper mill to be manufactured back into paper. This is then used to manufacture innovative board products, which are sold to the Zimbabwean packaging industry.
